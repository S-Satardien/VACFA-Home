The Beginning

​​The Vaccines for Africa Initiative (VACFA) is a non-profit academic unit founded in 2009 by Professor Gregory Hussey, who is the Director. We are in the Department of Public Health and Family Medicine, within Faculty of Health Sciences at the University of Cape Town..

Meet the Team

Our exceptional team at VACFA is driven by passion, expertise, and a shared dedication to fostering a healthier continent through vaccination. With distinguished researchers, healthcare professionals, and visionary leaders, we curate cutting-edge resources and provide comprehensive training to empower individuals across Africa. As a leading destination for knowledge, innovation, and progress in immunization, we are committed to shaping a brighter, healthier future for Africa and beyond through education and practical expertise. Together, we make a lasting impact, one that positively transforms healthcare across the continent.
