The Beginning

​​The Vaccines for Africa Initiative (VACFA) is a non-profit academic unit founded in 2009 by Professor Gregory Hussey, who is the Director. We are in the Department of Public Health and Family Medicine, within Faculty of Health Sciences at the University of Cape Town.

Meet the Team

At VACFA, our exceptional team is passionately dedicated to fostering a healthier Africa through vaccination. With distinguished researchers, healthcare professionals, and visionary leaders, we curate cutting-edge resources and provide comprehensive training to empower individuals across the continent.
